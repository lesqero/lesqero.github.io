Why are we Resisting?

The joint crisis of Capitalism and Colonialism continues to destroy millions of lives across the world. We see the symptoms of these systems in the rising death toll from the climate crisis, the unequal accumulation of wealth by just a few and the stark brutality of neocolonialism from South America to Palestine!
