We are

Youth in resistance

A community of Radical Education and Direct Action. We unite to resist the institutions and oppressive structures that exploit people and planet around the world.

We foster a strong community based on the principles of joy, trust and empowerment. This community will support itself and commit to collectively learn how to resist. We will translate our community learning into effective direct action.

Why are we Resisting?

The joint crisis of Capitalism and Colonialism continues to destroy millions of lives across the world.

We see the symptoms of these systems in the rising death toll from the climate crisis, the unequal accumulation of wealth by just a few and the stark brutality of neocolonialism from South America to Palestine!
